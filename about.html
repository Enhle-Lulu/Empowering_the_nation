About Empowering the Nation

Transforming lives through quality skills training since 2022

Our Mission

Founded on a Vision to Empower

In 2022, Precious Radebe founded Empowering the Nation with a clear mission: to provide accessible, high-quality skills training to domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg.

Recognizing the skills gap and limited opportunities available to these hardworking individuals, Precious created a training initiative that would help them gain valuable certifications, enhance their career prospects, and potentially start their own businesses.
