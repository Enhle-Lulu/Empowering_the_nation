Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering the Nation was created to address the skills gap among domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to improve their career prospects.

Our comprehensive training programmes have helped hundreds of individuals gain valuable certifications, secure better employment, and even start their own businesses.
